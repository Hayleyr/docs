## Summary

The most important part of maps is often your ability to share them. One way we make sharing maps easy with CartoDB is by giving you tools to easily add published visualizations to your blog posts. In this tutorial, we will show you in just a few steps how to add your CartoDB visualization to your Tumblr blog.
